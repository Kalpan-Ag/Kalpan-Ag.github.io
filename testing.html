Little about me and my experience

I am pursuing my Master's in Game Science and Design at Northeastern University. Albeit having a strong programming background, my interests and motivations lie in the Game Development and Design , particularly Virtual Reality.
For me, to be a CREATOR is to be someone who can interact between the learning environments, both physical and digital, with the attitudes and attributes of both masters and beginners, and produce a clear problem-solving process for a perceptible product. I am always curious and enjoy learning about new things. I like to be on my feet and keep myself fascinated at all times. Utilizing technology and every opportunity to learn and grow, I aim to eventually work on Full Immersion Virtual Reality.
